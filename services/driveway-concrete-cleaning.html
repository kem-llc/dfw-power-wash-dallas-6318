Driveway & Concrete Cleaning

Remove tough stains and grime from driveways, walkways, and patios.

Overview

Our hot water pressure washing system blasts away oil, grease, and embedded dirt, restoring your concrete to its original color. We treat all surfaces gently to avoid damage, leaving a clean, even finish.

What's included

  • Eliminates oil stains and tire marks
  • No etching or surface damage
  • Enhances curb appeal and property value
  • Safe for surrounding landscaping

How it works

1

Inspection & Pre-Treatment

Common questions

Will pressure washing damage my driveway?

No, when done correctly. Our technicians use the proper pressure and technique to clean without etching or cracking. We adjust settings based on concrete condition.

How often should I have my driveway cleaned?

We recommend every 1-2 years to prevent stain buildup and maintain appearance. High-traffic areas may benefit from annual cleaning.
